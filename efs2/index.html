Take Flight in a Cessna 172

About

I provide one-on-one flight training in my Cessna 172 in Las Vegas. Whether you are pursuing your Sport, Private, or Instrument rating, I emphasize personal attention and real-world skills to help you become a safe and confident pilot.

Services

Light Sport Pilot

FAR Part 61 — minimum 20 hours required. Must be 17+, hold a valid driver's license, and pass the FAA written exam.

  • 20 hrs Cessna 172 "wet" @ $160/hr — $3,200
  • Instructor: $60/hr (flight or ground)
  • Aircraft insurance: $115/month
  • FAA written exam: ~$180 (paid to test facility)

Private Pilot

FAR Part 61 — minimum 40 hours required. Must be 17+ and able to read, speak, and understand English.

  • 40 hrs Cessna 172 "wet" @ $160/hr — $6,400
  • Instructor: $60/hr (flight or ground)
  • Aircraft insurance: $115/month
  • FAA written exam: ~$180 (paid to test facility)
  • FAA examiner fee: ~$1,000 (paid to examiner)

Instrument Rating

FAR Part 61 — requires Private Pilot certificate and at least a Third Class Medical.

  • 40 hrs Cessna 172 "wet" @ $175/hr — $7,000
  • Instructor: $65/hr (flight or ground)
  • Aircraft insurance: $115/month
  • FAA examiner fee: ~$1,000 (paid to examiner)

Other Services

BFRs, IPC checks, and owner-aircraft training available.

  • Owner aircraft instructor rate: $75/hr
  • Instructor insurance (if needed): $120/month
  • Owner must carry aircraft insurance listing instructor as additionally insured
